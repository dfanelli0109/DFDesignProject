ABOUT DANIEL

BIO

From even my younger days, I have always had an eye for creativity. My interests have evolved over the years from being behind a camera, editing, journalism, and problem solving into web development. In high school and at the University of Central Florida, I studied digital communications, advertising, business management/accounting and took my first programming class in Pascal. After graduating from college, I owned a special events and wedding videography company, Majestic Moments Digital Studios, where I had the opportunity to maintain my company website and get familiar with some of the front end languages and search engine optimization.

In 2017, I was introduced to an opportunity to learn web development and with a wife newly pregnant, I jumped at the opportunity. At Woz-U, I completed a program and earned a Full Stack Software Engineer certificate. I am ready to put my skills to work for you. If you are looking for a website or if your website isn't bringing in the traffic that you would like, please contact me for a free consultation.
